Feta Cheese & Spinach Quiche



Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 (10-ounce) package of frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
  • 1 (4.5-ounce) canned mushrooms, drained
  • 1 (6-ounce) package of herb and garlic feta, crumbled
  • 1 (8-ounce) package of shredded cheddar, divided
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 (9-inch) deep-die pie shell
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup of milk


Method


Step 1

Preheat the oven to 191 degrees celsius


Step 2

Add butter to a medium skillet over medium heat. Once melted, add the onion and garlic. Stir until the onion is translucent, about 7 minutes.


Step 3

Mix in the spinach, mushrooms, feta, and 1/2 cup of cheddar. Season with salt and pepper.


Step 4

Beat eggs and milk in a medium bowl. Season with salt and pepper.

Step 5

Spread the spinach filling evenly into the pie shell. Pour in the custard, allowing it to incorporate with the filling.


Step 6

Bake it for 15 minutes. Sprinkle the remaining cheddar on top. Bake it for 35 to 40 minutes more, or until the filling is set. Allow the pie to cool for 10 minutes before serving. Slice it and enjoy!
